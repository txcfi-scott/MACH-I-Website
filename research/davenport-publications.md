## Major Publication Series: NATO Aviation Cardiology

### Heart Journal (2018-2019) — NATO HFM-251 Working Group

A landmark **10-paper series** from the NATO Occupational Aviation Cardiology Working Group, a 3-year international collaborative effort. Eddie served as U.S. delegate and prior chairman.

1. **"An introduction to aviation cardiology"** — Heart, Jan 2019
2. **"Assessing aeromedical risk: a three-dimensional risk matrix approach"** — Heart, Jan 2019
3. **"Management of established coronary artery disease in aircrew without myocardial infarction or revascularisation"** — Heart, Jan 2019
4. **"Management of established coronary artery disease in aircrew with previous myocardial infarction or revascularisation"** — Heart, Nov 2018
5. **"Management of cardiac conduction abnormalities and arrhythmia in aircrew"** — Heart, Jan 2019
6. **"Heart muscle disease management in aircrew"** — Heart, Nov 2018
7. **"Contemporaneous management of valvular heart disease and aortopathy in aircrew"** — Heart, Nov 2018
8. **"Congenital heart disease in aircrew"** — Heart, Jan 2019
9. **"Non-coronary cardiac surgery and percutaneous cardiology procedures in aircrew"** — Heart, Jan 2019
10. **"The challenge of asymptomatic coronary artery disease in aircrew; detecting vulnerable plaque before the accident"** — Heart, 2018
