Half-Day Program

The most thorough cardiovascular evaluation available outside a major academic medical center — comparable to what you would receive at Mayo Clinic, Cleveland Clinic, or Harvard — conducted by the only physician in the country who simultaneously serves as an FAA cardiovascular consultant, NASA consultant, and NATO aerospace medicine expert.
